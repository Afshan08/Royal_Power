Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our motorcycles and services.

Where can I buy Royal Power motorcycles?
You can purchase our motorcycles from any authorized dealer across Pakistan. Use our Dealer Locator to find the one nearest to you.
What is the warranty period?
We offer a 1-year or 12,000 km engine warranty (whichever comes first) on all our models, ensuring peace of mind with your purchase.
Do you offer installment plans?
Installment plans vary by dealer. Please contact your nearest authorized dealer for available financing options.
How can I book a service appointment?
You can visit any of our authorized 3S dealerships (Sales, Service, Spare Parts) for maintenance. No prior appointment is strictly necessary, but it is recommended to avoid waiting.
